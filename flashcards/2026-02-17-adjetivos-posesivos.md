## Card 7
id: fc-20260217-120500-007
anki_id:
tags: grammar, possessives, accent
type: basic

**Front:** What is the difference between **tú** and **tu**?

**Back:**
**Tú** (with accent) = You (Subject Pronoun). *Tú eres alto.*
**Tu** (no accent) = Your (Possessive Adjective). *Tu casa es grande.*

**Front:** The adjective **su/sus** can mean multiple things. Name three.

**Back:**
1. His (de él)
2. Her (de ella)
3. Your formal (de usted)
4. Their (de ellos/ellas)
5. Your plural (de ustedes)
